Smash Bros Ultimate character reveals before Steve

Here are the last 3 charechters to be revealed before Steve:

  1. Min min

    Min min is a character from Nintendo's ARMs.

    • Min min was debuted in a trailer titled "Ramen time!"
    • In the epilogue of her trailer Min min is seen enjoying ramen with Kirby
    • Min min is the only fighter in the game to hit with right and left arms instead of attack and special attack

  2. Byleth

    Byleth is the main protagonist from Nintendo's Fire Emblem: Three Houses

    • Byleth debuted in a trailer titled "The Ashen Demon"
    • Byleth is one of six Fire Emblem characters in Smash Bros Ultimate
    • The addition of Byleth to Smash Ultimate seemed repetitive to portions of the Smash community,
      with even director Masahiro Sakurai admitting there are too many swordfighters in Smash Ultimate

  3. Terry

    Terry is the main protagonist from SNK's Fatal Fury

    • Terry debuted in a trailer titled "-Breaking News- The Challenger"
    • Terry is a melee-focused, heavyweight character with immense combo, damage, and KO options
    • Terry was the first fighter from any of SNK's games
